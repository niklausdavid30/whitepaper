Announcing Our Vision for Recognition and Founders NFTs!

We’re excited to share a new initiative designed to foster innovation, celebrate contributions, and create a collaborative culture within our team.

Going forward, every successful invention or project will recognize two key contributors as Founders:

  • The Designer - The visionary behind the idea who conceptualizes and designs it.
  • The Implementer - The dedicated individual who brings the idea to life through execution.

Special Recognition

Each Founder will be awarded a unique Founder NFT, representing their invaluable contribution. These NFTs will serve as a lasting token of appreciation, ensuring both creative and practical efforts are acknowledged.

Leadership Structure

After recognizing 82 Founders, the person with the most Founder NFTs will take on leadership responsibilities, establishing a merit-based system for driving future success!

This initiative is about celebrating our team’s brilliance, encouraging creativity, and empowering every individual to shine. Let’s innovate, collaborate, and make history together!
