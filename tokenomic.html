Peremoga PMC
Tokenomics Policy

Total Supply

The Peremoga Token is designed to support the operational and f inancial needs of Peremoga PMC, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and sustainability in funding military operations, research, and veteran support.

  • Total Supply: 5,000,000,000 Peremoga Tokens
  • Initial Mint: 1,500,000,000 Peremoga Tokens

    • Fund Allocation

    The initial mint of 1,500,000,000 tokens will be distributed across four primary funds, ensuring strategic growth and operational efficiency. These funds are structured to support development, innovation, and veteran support, aligning with Peremoga PMC's mission.

    Allocation Breakdown

    Fund Type Initial Allocation(Tokens) Description
    Operations Fund 375,000,000 Covers active military operations, logistics,
    salaries, and equipment maintenance.
    Research & Development (R&D) Fund 375,000,000 Supports AI-driven battlefield automation, drone warfare
    advancements, and cyber infrastructure improvements.
    Medical Fund 375,000,000 Provides medical support, rehabilitation, and
    psychological care for active and retired PMC members.
    Veteran & Family Support Fund 375,000,000 Ensures financial aid, housing, and educational opportunities
    for veterans and the families of fallen operatives.

    Initial Sale & Fund Utilization

    A portion of the Operations Fund will be allocated for the initial sale of Peremoga Tokens, generating capital for mission-critical expenditures and furthering the PMC's strategic goals.

  • Tokens for Initial Sale: 20,000,000 (from Operations Fund).
  • Purpose: Raising funds for infrastructure, recruitment, and mission readiness.

    • Future Token Issuance & Governance

    The remaining 3,500,000,000 tokens will be locked and gradually issued based on:

  • Operational needs
  • Mission expansions
  • Governance decisions by the Peremoga PMC Collective Council

    • Governance Mechanism

    A decentralized voting system among PMC members will determine future token releases and fund allocations.

    Token Unlock Schedule

    Controlled via smart contracts to ensure transparency and prevent unauthorized minting.

    Conclusion

    The Peremoga Tokenomics model ensures that all funds are allocated with maximum efficiency and accountability. With a decentralized structure and blockchain-based financial management, Peremoga PMC maintains financial integrity while sustaining its mission to revolutionize AI-driven military operations.
