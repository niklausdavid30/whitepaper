Tokenomics Policy

Version: 1.0 | February 2025

This document outlines the tokenomics policy for Peremoga PMC, defining how employees are compensated using Peremoga Tokens (PRM) and the HelpFromAbove Tokens (HFA)...

Payment Structure

Compensation in Peremoga Tokens (PRM)

  • Employees and contractors will be paid in PRM.
  • 1 PRM = 1 DJED during ICO.
  • Payments executed via blockchain-based smart contracts.

Smart Contract-Based Governance

Payroll Automation

  • Payments processed via Plutus smart contracts.
  • Unauthorized transactions blocked automatically.

Future Adjustments

This policy is subject to updates based on DAO governance decisions, market conditions, and PMC operational expansions.
